Siege of ChangChun documentary

The Siege of Changchun was a military blockade undertaken by the People’s Liberation Army against the Republic of China Army, it was one of the longest military operations during the Liao-Shen Campaign of the Chinese Civil War.

From 10/1947 to 10/1948, Changchun had slowly became a "dead city”, at least 120,000 civilians starved to death. Famous writer LongYingtai stated the casualties may up to 700,000.

But this part of history is strange to people, and existing small-scale documents hold different stands.

To find out the unclear history, to record the fading evidence, we strived to found eight people who survived during that time and willing to talk.

The video editing is in still on-going, here I have chosen the keyframes trying to draw you a broad outline.
